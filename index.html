About us

Radrone is a scientific project founded in 2020 by a group of young people. Since its inception, the main goal of our team is to develop devices that are useful to mankind, bringing innovations in the field of radiation monitoring and radiation safety. One of the main problems Radrone solves is the exposure of people who monitor radiation to radioactive substances. The main difference between the device, which is the first of its kind in Azerbaijan, and global projects is that it accurate, qualitative, and cheaper.
